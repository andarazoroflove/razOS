Red Beard on Gold Hunt v.1.0 - May 7, 2006
------------------------------------------
An "Original Mario"-style game for eyeOS, featuring the
flash character, Red Beard.  Automatically saves your
progress as you play, so every time you open the game,
you have the option of resuming your previous game.

This game uses Flash, and therefore an installed copy of
the Flash plugin is required for your browser before this
game will run.


Known Bugs:
- None known.
