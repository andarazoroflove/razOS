Prince of Persia v.1.0 - May 7, 2006
------------------------------------
The classic Prince of Persia game for eyeOS.  Bring back
memories of the first well-animated game, and enjoy the
timeless action of the original Prince of Persia game.

This game uses Flash, and therefore an installed copy of
the Flash plugin is required for your browser before this
game will run.

- None known.
