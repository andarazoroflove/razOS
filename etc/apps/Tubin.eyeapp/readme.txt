Tubin' on Lake Tyler v.1.0 - May 7, 2006
----------------------------------------
A flash game where you guide the tuber riding behind a boat.
Lots of obstacles and fun sound effects.

Tubin' uses Flash, and therefore an installed copy of the
Flash plugin is required for your browser before Tubin'
will run.


Known Bugs:
- None known.
