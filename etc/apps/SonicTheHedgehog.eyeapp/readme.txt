Ultimate Flash Sonic v.1.0 - May 7, 2006
----------------------------------------
A Flash game based on the classic Sonic The Hedgehog games.

Ultimate Flash Sonic uses Flash, and therefore an installed
copy of the Flash plugin is required for your browser before
Ultimate Flash Sonic will run.
